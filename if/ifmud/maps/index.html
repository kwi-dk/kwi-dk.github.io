ifMUD maps

Iain says, "It's nice to know that we have hundreds of bizarre rooms to explore in the 0.001% of the time we're not in the lounge."

When I made these maps in the fall of 2003, there were 1739 rooms on the ifMUD, of which these maps cover about 200. The maps were never quite completed, and are now without doubt out of date. They remain here mostly as a curiosity.

Other sources of ifMUD maps include Tyson Boucher's map page and David Welbourn's Cosmic Encounters maps.
